Left Clicker:
l (or) lcm (or) left <avg> <cps> - Change your cps for left mouse click. (Form 0 to 25)
l (or) lcm (or) left <gam> <bool> - Change the left mouse button settings to disable the ability to click outside of the game. (True or False)
